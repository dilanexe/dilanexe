## Why This Palette Works for Sri Lankan Urban Audience

1. **Premium Positioning**: The combination of deep teal and gold signals luxury and professional medical expertise - key factors for affluent, health-conscious urban professionals in Sri Lanka.

2. **Cultural Resonance**: 
   - Teal/turquoise connects to Sri Lanka's coastal identity and tropical landscape
   - Gold reflects traditional South Asian appreciation for precious metals and luxury
   - The color combination evokes both modern sophistication and cultural authenticity

3. **Trust & Credibility**: Deep teal is scientifically proven to increase trust and is widely used in healthcare and premium beauty brands globally.

4. **Urban Appeal**: The bright cyan accents provide a contemporary, forward-thinking feel that appeals to tech-savvy urban professionals.

5. **Accessibility**: High contrast ratios between the primary teal and white/light backgrounds ensure excellent readability and WCAG AA compliance.

## Application Throughout the Site

- **Navigation Header**: Teal background with gold accents
- **Call-to-Action Buttons**: Teal primary buttons with gold hover states
- **Service Cards**: Teal borders, gold highlights
- **Hero Section**: Teal accent elements with gold typography
- **Form Elements**: Teal focus states, gold success states
- **Footer**: Teal background with gold link accents

This palette creates a cohesive, premium, and culturally resonant brand identity that appeals directly to Elite Derma Care's target market of affluent, urban Sri Lankan clients seeking high-end medical aesthetic treatments.
