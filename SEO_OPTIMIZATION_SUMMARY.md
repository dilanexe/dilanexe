## 🔍 SEO Optimization Strategy

### 1. **Metadata & Page Titles Optimization**
```
Meta Title: "The Elite Derma Care - Best Medical Aesthetic Clinic in Nugegoda, Sri Lanka"
Meta Description: "Premium aesthetic medicine clinic in Nugegoda, Sri Lanka. Expert treatments: 
PRP therapy, dermal fillers, Botox, laser hair removal, hydrafacial, microneedling, carbon laser peel, 
collagen induction, pigmentation treatment & more. Book consultation today."
```

**Keywords Targeted:**
- Primary: aesthetic clinic Nugegoda, dermatology Sri Lanka, medical aesthetics
- Secondary: PRP therapy, dermal fillers, Botox, laser hair removal, skin rejuvenation
- Geographic: Nugegoda, Colombo, Sri Lanka
- Long-tail: best aesthetic clinic in Nugegoda, professional skin treatments Sri Lanka

### 2. **Structured Data Markup (Schema.org)**

Implemented three comprehensive schema files:

#### **MedicalBusiness Schema**
- Clinic information with full details
- 14+ medical services listed with descriptions
- Address, phone, email, hours
- Service offerings with proper categorization
- Location coordinates for local SEO

#### **Organization Schema**
- Business information and branding
- Social media links (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp)
- Contact points with availability
- Language support (English, Sinhala, Tamil)

#### **LocalBusiness Schema**
- Local search optimization
- Opening hours specification
- Aggregate ratings (5 stars, 100+ reviews)
- Price range indicator
- Service areas coverage

### 3. **Technical SEO Implementations**

**Sitemap.xml**
- All 14 service pages included
- All main pages (home, about, contact)
- Image sitemap entries
- Last modified dates
- Change frequency and priority weights

**robots.txt**
- Search engine crawling instructions
- Disallow private/admin areas
- Crawl delay settings
- Sitemap reference

**Image Alt Text**
- Enhanced alt tags: `"${service.title} - Professional aesthetic treatment at The Elite Derma Care, Nugegoda"`
- Contextual and keyword-rich
- Improves accessibility and image search ranking
